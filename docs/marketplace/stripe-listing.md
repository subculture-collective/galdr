## Short Description

Customer health scores and churn alerts powered by Stripe billing data.

## Long Description

PulseScore helps B2B SaaS teams spot churn risk earlier by turning Stripe billing activity into clear customer health scores. Connect Stripe with read-only OAuth, import customers, subscriptions, invoices, and payments, then review every account through health scoring, risk segmentation, customer detail timelines, and alert workflows.

The app highlights payment recency, failed-payment risk, MRR movement, account activity, and support signals so customer success and revenue teams can prioritize the accounts that need action before renewal risk becomes visible in revenue. Teams can view portfolio health, drill into a customer detail page, and create email alerts when scores drop or customers move into an at-risk tier.

PulseScore is built for SaaS operators who already run billing through Stripe and need a simple customer-health layer without spreadsheets or custom scripts. The Stripe connection is read-only; PulseScore cannot create charges, issue refunds, modify subscriptions, or access banking data.

## Benefits

- Turn Stripe billing events into a 0-100 health score for each customer.
- Prioritize green, yellow, and red risk segments from one dashboard.
- Open a customer detail view to see subscriptions, recent events, payment health, and score drivers.
- Trigger alerts when a health score drops or a customer enters an at-risk tier.
- Use read-only Stripe OAuth so PulseScore can analyze billing data without payment write access.

## How It Works

1. Install PulseScore from the Stripe App Marketplace.
2. Authorize read-only Stripe access through OAuth.
3. PulseScore imports Stripe customers, subscriptions, invoices, and payment history.
4. Health score factors calculate payment recency, MRR trend, failed-payment risk, and customer activity.
5. Teams review dashboard segments, customer detail timelines, and alerts to take action.
